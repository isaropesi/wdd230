Southeast Brazil Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name for this chamber of commerce website is "Southeast Brazil Chamber of Commerce." This name represents the geographical region of focus and the organization's purpose of promoting local businesses and economic growth in the southeast of Brazil.

Site Purpose

The site will serve two main purposes:

  • As a regional economic catalyst, bringing businesses together and fostering economic and community development in the southeast region of Brazil.
  • As a platform to promote business networking, provide information about the region's attractions, and facilitate community engagement.

Scenarios

The website will address the following scenarios for our target audience:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the recent population growth in the southeast region of Brazil?

Color Schema

The color scheme will reflect the region's identity and purpose:

  • Primary Color: #16A086
  • Secondary Color: #C3A657
  • Accent Color: #000000
  • Background Color: #FFFFFF

Typography

Typography choices to provide a consistent and professional look:

  • Heading Font: "Work Sans", sans-serif (Used for headings and subheadings)
  • Body Font: "Raleway", sans-serif (Used for body text)
